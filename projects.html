GRIDOT

Optimal Transport + Graph-based Granger Causality for GRN inference

Optimal Transport GNN Multi-omics Granger
▾

Gene regulatory network inference using Optimal Transport alignment coupled with graph-based Granger causality to recover directed regulatory interactions across conditions and time.

  • Aligns distributions across conditions to stabilize downstream causal estimation.
  • Infers directed edges with interpretable causal scores.
  • Designed for noisy, high-dimensional omics regimes.

Foundation Models Spatial Omics Diffusion
▾

Diffusion-based Representation Integration for Foundation Models in Spatial Transcriptomics: improves the coherence of embeddings and downstream spatial tasks using diffusion-driven smoothing and integration.
